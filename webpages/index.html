Recent Outreaches and Activities

Outreaches for this period are coming to a closure. It was quite an interesting at the same time exciting to visit several villages at different geopolitical zones in the country Nigeria. Some villagers who have had to struggle for basic amenities in health, infrastructure, education, electricity amongst others, have their hopes were revived as we shared the Love of Christ with them. They were attended to spiritually, medically and physically. Our team consisting of professionals from different fronts of life, in whose heart the Love of God and of His Kingdom abide made themselves available just to put smiles on the faces of both young and old in these far palces. Locations visited includes:

  • Abuja FCT
  • Kebbi state
  • Gombe state
  • Taraba state
  • Kwara state
  • Shaki state
  • Ogun state

and many more...
